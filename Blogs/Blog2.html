Reflection on Moulthrop, S. (2003) reading:

The text examines the pros and cons while talking about the development of the hypertext. I am going to examine this text on hypertext while linking it to modern day internet and how we interact with it. The hypertext offers the connections for researchers to find information text (Moulthrop, 2003, p. 693). In modern day internet a lot of people use the hypertext for many things such as research, or just for personal use. Many university students use the hypertext for project that they are working on. They would then add links to these websites in their research so that people reading their work can find the original peace of information. The origin of hypertext has evolved so much that we even use it as media, and which include graphics and videos. Not only do we have text being linked to other text, but we also now have text being linked to images on the webs. The internet as we know it today is basically built around some form of hypertext because we use this to navigate the web as well.
