Games

Lab Rumble

Lab Rumble is a 2.5D game where players navigate a laboratory filled with potions and Robots. The Lab is about to explode so the players need to fight each other by throwing potions at each other so that the last one alive can escape on the rocket. Try not get caught on the explosions and avoid the robots!

Play on Itch.io

Rotus Compltus

Rotus Compltus is a 2D top down Tile placement game where two players compete to connect their opposite sections. Make sure to get the longest path as well to get more points!

Play on Itch.io

Unbound Echos

Unbound Echos was my second year semester 2 game. It is a 3D first person horror shootinggame where the player is trapped inside a house filled with monsters. Find all the clues to escape and try not to get caught by the monsters.
