About Me

I'm Connor Van Heerden, a game designer and develper passionate about crafting unique and playful digital experiences. I'm currently a 3rd-year student at the University of the Witwatersrand, pursuing a BA in Digital Arts with majors in Game Design and Interactive Media.

Outside of university, I'm the co-founder and lead game designer at Safe Dollar Studios, a gaming company focused on playful, experimental, and engaging projects. This website is a peak into the work I've done. From solo creations to collaborative efforts. Feel free to explore my portfolio to see some of the games I've designed or helped bring to life.
